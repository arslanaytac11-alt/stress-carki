Data Collection

Information We Collect

Stress Spinner does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. No account creation, login, or registration is required.

The following data is stored locally on your device only:

  • Game progress (level, XP, scores)
  • Daily quest status
  • Unlocked spinner collection items
  • App settings (language preference)

This data never leaves your device.

Advertising

Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect certain data as described in Google's Privacy Policy, including:

  • Device identifiers (advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • General location (country/region level)
  • App usage data for ad personalization

Opt Out of Personalized Ads

iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security > Apple Advertising > turn off Personalized Ads

Privacy Details

Children's Privacy

Stress Spinner is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Security

All game data is stored locally on your device, protected by your device's built-in security. We do not operate any servers or databases.

Data Deletion

You can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling the app. No data remains on any external server.
