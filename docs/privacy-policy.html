Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 3, 2026

Stress Spinner ("we", "our", or "the app") is a free stress relief mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

Stress Spinner does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. We do not require account creation, login, or any form of registration.

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Game progress (level, XP, scores)
  • Daily quest status
  • Unlocked spinner collection items
  • App settings (language preference)

This data is stored using the device's local storage and is never transmitted to any server.

2. Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect certain data as described in Google's Privacy Policy. This may include:

  • Device identifiers (advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • General location (country/region level)
  • App usage data for ad personalization

You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings:

  • iOS: Settings > Privacy > Apple Advertising > turn off Personalized Ads

3. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob — for displaying advertisements. Google Privacy Policy

4. Children's Privacy

Stress Spinner is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app contains advertisements that are not specifically targeted to children.

5. Data Security

Since all game data is stored locally on your device, your data is protected by your device's built-in security. We do not operate servers or databases that store user information.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

All app data is stored locally on your device. You can delete all data at any time by uninstalling the app. No data remains on any external server after deletion.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
